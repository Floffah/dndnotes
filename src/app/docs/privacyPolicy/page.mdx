# Privacy Policy

Preferred contact method: [Discord](https://discord.gg/DsSeGSc5na)

Please take some time to read the [Terms of Service](/docs/termsOfService) as it provides important information about your rights and obligations when using the service. It also defines definitions of terms used in this document.

## What information do we collect?

We collect information from you when you register on our site and gather data when you participate in the forum by reading, writing, and evaluating the content shared here.

When registering on our site, we will collect information provided by the OAuth provider *Discord* (authorised by you by clicking "Login With Discord"). This is limited to: Discord ID, username, and email.

When registered and interacting with this site, your IP Address is not stored, but we may retain server logs which include the IP address of every request to our backend.

When providing feedback or suggestions to us, we may collect your Discord ID, username, and email.

Other non-identifying information may be collected.
This includes but is not limited to:
- Campaigns you have created or joined and the data associated with them you have provided
- Friends you have added on the platform
- Your profile information (e.g. bio, avatar, etc.) - this has not been implemented yet, this is limited to username and email although only username is made public
- Your feedback and suggestions

## What do we use your information for?

Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:

- To personalize your experience — your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs.
- To improve our site — we continually strive to improve our site offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you.
- To improve customer service — your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs.

There are more ways that are planned but not yet implemented.

### Future plans

- To send periodic emails — The email address you provide may be used to send you information, notifications that you request about changes to topics, responses to inquiries, and/or other requests or questions.
- Voice chat - We may offer an in-site voice chat system which can optionally be recorded (but is otherwise not)

## How do we protect your information?

All data is stored in [MongoDB Atlas](https://www.mongodb.com/atlas/database), which is a cloud database service. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. All data is backed up daily and stored in multiple locations by the provider.

Our backend, database, and any internal services are NOT hosted by us and are only accessible by the owner of the project.

We use an environment secret service from [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) to store our [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/) connection string. This is not stored in the repository and is only accessible by the owner of the project.

## Do we use cookies?

Yes. However limited to the following:
- Session token - used to authenticate you to the site

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes nor embed any outside provider with access to the above cookie.

## Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our site, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. These third-parties are: [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/) (stores our Database), [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) (hosts the site and backend). We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety.
